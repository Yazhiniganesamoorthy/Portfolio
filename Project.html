Image Transmission Using Under Water Wireless Optical Communication

Technologies used: Wireless Optical Communication

• Underwater wireless optical communication (UWOC) enables image transmission using light. LEDs or lasers encode data; photodetectors capture signals. UWOC revolutionizes underwater data transfer, promising speed and reliability.

• This novel approach promises unparalleled clarity and reliability in underwater environments, unlocking new possibilities for exploration, surveillance, and scientific research in the depths of our oceans. It includes developing compression methods suitable for optical channels, adapting modulation techniques to underwater conditions, and simulating channel behavior.
